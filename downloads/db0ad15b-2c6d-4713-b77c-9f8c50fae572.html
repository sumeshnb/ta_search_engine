Undergraduate Applicants

The CSE Department of UTA offers three ABET Accredited Bachelor's Degree Programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering
  • Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering
  • Student may also minor in Computer Science.

To learn more about our undergraduate programs, visit our Undergraduate Study page. 

To apply, please visit the UTA Admissions website. The department does not have any additional admission requirements, and is not involved with the admission process.

Scholarships and Financial Aid

You may apply for one or more of the general academic scholarships offered by the university during your initial application process. Visit the UTA Scholarships and Financial Aid Office for more information. The College of Engineering also has a few scholarships for undergraduates. Once you are admitted and enrolled into UTA, there are jobs and internships opportunities at the university. Furthermore, every year the Department of Computer Science and Engineering awards departmental scholarships to a few outstanding students. (Reference: Registration Costs & Payments)

About Arlington

Our location in the nation's second-largest technology corridor - the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex - and strong relationship with leading technology companies provide you with great opportunities for internships and jobs. Arlington, home to UTA, is centrally located between Dallas and Fort Worth and abounds with recreational activities. It is a fun and exciting place to live and attend school. Experience Arlington!

Great futures begin here. Want to know more about living in north Texas? Visit our campus.
