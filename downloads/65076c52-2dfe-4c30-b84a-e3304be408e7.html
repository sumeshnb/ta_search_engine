Students are advised by different offices, based on their status. Please read all the options below:

  • Freshman students – University Advising Center
  • Students on probation and students who had a GPA of less than 2.5 when admitted – Beth Isbell (office: NH 242, email: erisbell@uta.edu, phone: 817-272-1294)
  • Transfer students and continuing students in good standing – CSE Undergraduate advisors, on a walk-in basis. See the Advising Calendar below for walk-in hours.
    • Transfer students seeking to transfer a course taken at any college or university that is international, outside of Texas, or a 4-year institution in Texas, must see Dr. Barasch and request a course evaluation.
    • Transfer students coming from a 2-year institution from Texas can see any of the CSE Undergraduate advisors.
